Privacy Policy

Who We Are

BDE Social is a creative digital agency based in Canada offering social media marketing, content creation, and brand development services. Our website address is: bdesocial.ca.

What Personal Data We Collect & Why

  • Contact forms: When you fill out a form, we collect your name, email, and message to follow up on your inquiry.
  • Analytics: We use tools like Google Analytics to understand how visitors interact with our website and improve user experience.
  • Cookies: Cookies help us track performance and personalize your experience. You can disable them in your browser settings.
  • Social Logins or Embeds: Content from platforms like Instagram, TikTok, Pinterest, or Meta may track user interaction. These platforms have their own privacy policies.

How We Use Your Information

We use your data to respond to inquiries, deliver services, analyze website performance, and manage client relationships. We never sell your information to third parties.

Third-Party Services

We may integrate tools like Instagram, Pinterest, Meta Ads, or TikTok Analytics on our site. These tools may collect anonymous usage data under their own privacy terms.

Data Protection & Security

We follow industry best practices to secure your data — including encrypted storage, restricted access, and regular site maintenance. However, no digital transmission is 100% secure.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal information. You can email us at info@bdesocial.ca with any data-related requests.

Cookies

This site may store cookies on your device to enhance performance and personalize your experience. You can choose to disable cookies via your browser settings.

How Long We Retain Data

If you send a message through our contact form or become a client, your information may be retained for as long as needed to manage the working relationship, or until you request deletion.

Policy Updates

This Privacy Policy may be updated occasionally to reflect changes in our practices. Last updated: July 2025.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about how we handle your data, email us anytime at info@bdesocial.ca.
