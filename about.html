About Us

We’re sisters by chance, co-founders by choice, and dreamers by design.

BDE Social is built on the bond we’ve shared since childhood—a connection rooted in creativity, laughter, and an unshakable belief in each other. What began as casual brainstorming over coffee turned into a full-fledged agency where passion meets precision, and aesthetics meet strategy.

Vruti Bachwani

Co-Founder, Strategy & Storytelling Lead

Vruti is the strategist and steady pulse behind BDE Social. With a strong background in accounting and years of hands-on experience in finance, she approaches every brand challenge with clarity, logic, and thoughtful structure. She’s not just about the numbers—she’s about people, consistency, and building businesses that last.

Her mind naturally leans toward systems and long-term vision, making her the go-to for internal planning, client direction, and scaling with intention. Whether it’s crafting proposals, running operations, or mapping our next big move, she brings a sense of calm and confidence to every project.

Grounded, graceful, and endlessly dedicated, Vruti keeps us aligned, accountable, and always growing forward—together.

Khushi Bachwani

Co-Founder, Creative Director & Visual Strategist

Where Vruti grounds the vision, Khushi gives it wings. A design soul through and through, Khushi is all about aesthetics that speak—not just look good. With a pulse on trends and a deep understanding of what makes people stop and feel, she crafts visuals that tell stories, stir emotions, and convert audiences into loyal communities.

With a background in IT and digital editing, Khushi brings a rare blend of logic and creativity. She’s not just about how things look—she understands how they work. Whether she's coding a website, optimizing layouts, or fine-tuning video edits, her tech fluency and artistic eye work in harmony.

Khushi is intuitive, bold, and fiercely passionate about helping brands find their unique voice. She leads with her heart, connects with her gut, and never shies away from doing things differently if it means doing them better.

Together at BDE

We balance each other like strategy balances creativity. Our working style is rooted in respect, trust, and a shared obsession with detail. We don’t just build brands—we build relationships, pouring our full selves into every project because that’s the only way we know how to work.

BDE Social isn’t just a business. It’s our way of helping others shine online, show up with confidence, and feel proud of what they put into the world.

Welcome to the energy.
